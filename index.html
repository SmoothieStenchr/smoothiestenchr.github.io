101 Smoothies


(Side note: To make these smoothies, add ice and xanthan gum (¼ tsp.) to the ingredients, then blend until extremely smooth-looking with no visible chunks. Each recipe makes 1 serving, you can scale them up to make more.)

Required Ingredients List

1. Tropical Freeze

Mango Juice (2½ oz.)
Orange Juice (2½ oz.)
Pineapple Juice (2½ oz.)
Lemon Juice (1 oz.)

2. Red Delicious

Apple Juice (⅔ cup)
Cranberry Juice (⅔ cup)
Apple Slices (½ apple)

3. CocoChoco

Crème de Coconut (20 g.)
Chocolate Syrup (20 g.)
Chocolate Protein Powder (2 Tbsp.)
Milk (¼ cup)
Water (¼ cup)
Coconut Milk (¼ cup)
SHAKE WELL

4. Coconut Apple Crumble

Apple Slices (½ apple)
Coconut Milk (1 cup)
Lime Juice (½ lime)
Crema di Pumpkin Pie (1 tsp.)
Cinnamon (¼ tsp.)
Pumpkin Pie Spice (¼ tsp.)

5. Milky Way

Milk (16 oz.)
Vanilla Greek Yogurt (2½ Tbsp.)
Chocolate Protein Powder (2 Tbsp.)
Unsweetened Cocoa (1 Tbsp.)
Almond Butter (16 g.)

6. Banana Bliss

Mashed Banana (1 banana)
Milk (¾ cup)
Apple Juice (⅓ cup)
Sugar (¼ tsp.)
Whipped Cream (3 g.)
Vanilla Powder (½ tsp.)

Crème de Coconut (?)

Lemon Juice (?)
Lime Juice (?)

Apple Juice (⅔ cup)
